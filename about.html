A few words about us

Africa is bedevilled by so many social, economic and political problems; Nigeria in particular has not escaped from this wealth of untold hardship. As a result of these difficulties, many have died, and many are dying. It is unfortunate to know however, that those who bear the brunt of all these sufferings are mothers and children and this is a situation the JEFFSON’S FOUNDATION is willing to change.

The JEFFSON’S FOUNDATION which is a non-governmental charity organization, kicked off with the main aim to cater for the Total well-being of the African child through Education and health services.

The JEFFSON’S FOUNDATION has a mission of organising constant free medical outreaches, organization of campaigns against emerging diseases and fight against HIV and AIDS in children and adolescent.

With a mission statement that reads “Everyone deserves to be happy”, the JEFFSON’S FOUNDATION has been able to organise seminars, workshops, trainings and walk campaigns to encourage more participation of youths in the organization.

The foundation which is presently located in Abuja and Ajayi Crowther University, Oyo, Nigeria, is set to expand its reach to other parts of the country and within record time other West African countries and Africa generally.
