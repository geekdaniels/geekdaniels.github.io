GRACE CHISOM NWOKIKE

GRACE CHISOM NWOKIKE is a graduate student at the prestigious Covenant University and a candidate for Masters degree in Environmental Chemistry. She had her Bachelor of Science degree also from Covenant University where she was selected to make a presentation on her project work in an International Conference and was awarded Best Poster Presentation. She was an ambassador of Face of Democracy Nigeria, a beauty pageant show designed to promote and encourage the Nigerian girl child and women participation in the growing democratic dispensation of Nigeria. Grace is a young lady whose vision is creating solutions to problems starting from putting smiles to people's faces in her little way to ending the world's greatest challenges at large. Her love for God is her driving force to showing love to others.

Olatunji Emmanuel Olayinka

Olatunji Emmanuel Olayinka, (PR/COMMUNICATION MANAGER) An undergraduate in school of communication and media studies, Ajayi Crowther University Oyo state, Nigeria. Emmanuel has work as an intern at Lasaco Assurance PLC, Ikeja Lagos state as well as Lagos traffic radio also based in Ikeja Lagos state. He is a student member of the Advertising practitioner council of Nigeria. He believes charity is the ability to use one's position of influence, relative wealth and power to affect lives for the better so as to make live healthy for all African Child.
