Gbenga Mathew Owotoki

Our Patron

Gbenga Mathew Owotoki is a transformational speaker, revivalist and author, base in Florida, USA. A US trained business and change management strategist widely known as the 'Change Driver' for his simple yet unique ways through which he initiates changes that are rejuvenating lives and organizations.

Many have testified to the glory of the for being used of the Lord to restore hope in individuals who had given up on life. He is a Pastor by Calling who has raised many disciples for the Lord and who in turn are doing great exploits for Jesus. He is the founder and Lead Pastor of Mountaintop Faith International Church and Presiding Coordinator of Hephzibah Network International Ministries committed to stirring up the 'sleeping giant' in people for end-time exploits.

After Owotoki graduated from University of Ilorin, he went ahead to study for his Masters of Science in Natural Resources Management at the University of Hohenheim, Stuttgart, Germany. He served also as the Pastor of New Covenant Church Stuttgart Germany and worked briefly for the Max Planck Institute for Biogeochemistry in Germany before moving on to London for further studies in Management. He later relocated to the US after receiving an offer for an Executive MBA degree in International Business at Schiller International University, Florida, USA.

While at Schiller, he was inducted into the Alfa-Beta honors association in the US and upon graduation; he received the Neil Armstrong award for outstanding performance in his graduate studies. He graduated top of his class. Gbenga is a member of some international organizations both in the US and Europe. He has further certification as a Business Analyst par excellence. Owotoki is in addition, a certified Human Resource specialist. Owotoki is also the Convener of the annual Giant Conference and Life Summit that have been a blessing to many lives.

He is married to His jewel of inestimable value, Eunice 'Rock Owotoki and they are blessed with two Sons, Leslie and Lemuel. gmattoki@gmail.com
